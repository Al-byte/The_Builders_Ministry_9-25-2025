William Branch

Will Branch is a former youth pastor whose calling has grown into a multifaceted mission: serving as a motivational speaker, spiritual mentor, worship leader, and history tour guide. With a powerful stage presence and a heart full of compassion, Will has led worship and spoken to thousands at churches, youth groups, camps, retreats, conferences, workshops, and campus ministry events across the country. His message is consistent and clear: it's not just about knowing information about God—it's about encountering Him.

Rooted in his deep faith and passion for people, Will’s life’s work revolves around helping individuals discover their God-given purpose. He inspires meaningful transformation by combining encouragement, empowerment, and intentional connection. Whether speaking to students, mentoring young leaders, or guiding a group through the landmarks of American history, Will’s authenticity and vision leave a lasting impression.

Will believes in championing purpose—he helps people uncover their identity and step into their calling with confidence. He encourages meaningful growth, turning challenges into opportunities, and has a gift for empowering self-belief, helping others see in themselves what they didn’t know was there. His strength lies in building intentional connections, strategically placing people in environments where they can thrive and live with impact.

Over the years, Will has served in a variety of influential roles:

  • Pastor of Middle School Ministry at Eastbrook Church (2009–2015)
  • Educational Program Leader with American Christian Tours (2015–present)
  • High School Program Coordinator at Elmbrook Church (2016–2019)
  • Campus Chaplain at Augustine Prep School (2019–2022)
  • Intercessory Missionary at the International House of Prayer (2022–present)
  • School Bus Driver/Trainer with Durham School Services (2023–present)

From ministry to mentoring, from education to worship leadership, Will Branch continues to be a passionate voice and faithful guide for those seeking a deeper connection with God and with their purpose.
